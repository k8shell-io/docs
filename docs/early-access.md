# Early Access

Some features in k8shell are available as **early access** — open to all users before they become part of a paid plan. Early access lets you start using new capabilities today and directly shape how they evolve.

## What does Early Access mean?

Early access features are:

- **Fully functional** — the core behaviour works, but APIs, configuration, or UX may change before GA.
- **Free to use now** — no billing or plan required during the early access period.
- **Eventually paid** — once the feature graduates to general availability it will become part of a paid or Pro plan.
- **Feedback-driven** — your input directly influences the final design.

## How to get Early Access

1. Join the [k8shell Slack](https://slack.k8shell.io) and head to the `#early-access` channel.
2. Express your interest and describe your use case.
3. The team will guide you through onboarding.

## Stability expectations

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Early Access** | Feature is usable; breaking changes possible; free to use |
| **GA / Pro** | Feature is stable, fully supported, and part of a paid plan |

Any feature marked with the **Early Access** badge in this documentation falls under the above terms.
